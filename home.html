OUR PRODUCTS:

DRIVEWAY GATES:

Our custom-made aluminum and wrought iron driveway gates combine strength and beauty. Available in various designs and finishes, these gates are tailored to meet your specific needs.


  • Aluminium Driveway Gates: Lightweight yet durable, aluminum gates offer long-lasting beauty without the risk of rust or corrosion.

Designed to match your driveway gates, our garden and walk-through gates can be customized with a variety of powder-coated finishes. Choose between wrought iron or aluminum to give your home a stylish, cohesive look.

Railings:

From balcony to front porch and deck railings, our options include metal, aluminum, and wrought iron. We offer custom-made, powder-coated designs for both residential and commercial locations, ensuring low maintenance and lasting quality.

Pool Fences:

Ensure safety and style with our aluminum pool fences and gates. Available in decorative or standard designs, these fences are ideal for both residential and commercial properties, meeting essential safety requirements.

Gate Operators and Openers:

We provide a variety of gate openers and operators for residential, commercial, and industrial gates. Our experts can help you choose the right system, including sliding, swing, or hydraulic gate openers. We also offer replacement parts, including circuit boards and remote controls.

WHY CHOOSE ALUMINUM?

  1. Cost: Aluminum gates are more affordable compared to steel gates, especially for elaborate designs.
  2. Lightweight: Lighter than steel, aluminum gates are easier to operate, especially for automatic gate systems.
  3. Durability: Aluminum does not rust or corrode, ensuring a longer-lasting, maintenance-free gate.
  4. Design Flexibility: Aluminum allows for thicker materials while remaining lightweight, providing more design and color options.
  5. Low Maintenance: Unlike steel, aluminum requires no sanding, priming, or repainting, saving you time and money in the long run.
